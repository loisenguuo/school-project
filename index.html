Getting Started

The Asset Inventory System helps you monitor and maintain a clear record of all your assets. With features like tracking, reporting, and customizable settings, it's designed to streamline asset management.

How It Works

  • Log in and navigate to the dashboard to view asset summaries.
  • Access detailed records of each asset and update information as needed.
  • Use the reports section to generate asset performance and status reports.

Next Steps
